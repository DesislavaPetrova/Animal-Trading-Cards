Giraffe

Giraffes only need 5 to 30 minutes of sleep in a 24-hour period! They often archieve that in quick naps that may last only a minute or two at a time.

  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Order: Artiodactyla
  • Family: Giraffidae

Giraffes are the tallest mammals on Earth. Their legs alone are taller than many humans - about 6 feet. No one has towered over these creatures since the time of the dinosaurs. Standing over 18 ft. tall, these creatures are long-necked and long-legged, making these lanky animals perfectly build for browsing on tall trees and branches, taking advantage of food sources other herbivores cannot.
